Seminar Series in Psychiatry and Behavioral Neuroscience 2022

Institute of Mental Health & Neurosciences (IMHANS), Kerala is organising a seminar series in the frontier areas of psychiatry and behaviour neurosciences through online platform. It’s got a page for registration, where you can embed a Google Form or some other way for participants to register. It’s got a program page to list the schedule for the conference. It’s got a directions page to add a bunch of instructions how to get to the conference. It’s got a page to put up the flyers and other promotional materials for folks to download.
