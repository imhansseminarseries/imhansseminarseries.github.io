Seminar Series in Psychiatry and Behavioral Neuroscience 2022

Research seminars help researchers to stay abreast of the most cutting-edge developments across the wide area of neuroscience. Institute of Mental Health & Neurosciences (IMHANS), Kerala, India is organising an online seminar series in the frontier areas of psychiatry and behaviour neurosciences. This flagship seminar series is held approximately once a month, inviting eminent researchers across the globe to share their expertise.

How to attend 2022

The online seminar series is open to the scientific community through online platform Zoom and also will be streamed in Youtube.

Organizers

Institute of Mental Health & Neurosciences (IMHANS) is an autonomous institution under the Government of Kerala and a Centre of Excellence in Mental Health located at Kozhikode district of Kerala state, India. Major focus of the institute is mental health care delivery, training, research and policy development.
